# D-Mart Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built using sales and customer data sourced from Excel.
It analyzes revenue, orders, discounts, cancellations, customer demographics, product performance, shipping modes, and payment methods to help understand overall business and customer behavior.

## Tech Stack

  * Data Source: Microsoft Excel
  * Data Visualization & Analysis: Power BI
  * Power BI Tools Used:
  * Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)
  * DAX measures
  * Interactive charts & KPIs
  * Slicers (Year-wise filtering)

## Features 

  * KPI cards showing Total Revenue, Total Orders, Total Discount, and Cancelled Orders
  * Year-wise slicer (2021â€“2023) for dynamic analysis
  * Monthly vs Yearly sales trend
  * Payment method breakdown (Debit, Credit, UPI, NetBanking, COD, Cancelled)
  * Age & gender distribution analysis
  * Shipping mode performance (Free, Priority, Express Plus)
  * Top revenue-generating products
  * Category-wise revenue split (Local, Branded, Imported)

## Goal of the Dashboard

  * The main goal of this dashboard is to:
  * Convert raw Excel data into meaningful business insights
  * Analyze customer behavior and purchasing patterns
  * Track sales trends, cancellations, and discounts
  * Support management in making data-driven decisions

## Key Insights

  * Total revenue generated is 16.95M
  * Debit and Credit cards are the most used payment methods
  * Male customers contribute a higher share compared to female customers
  * Free shipping is the most preferred delivery mode
  * A small set of products contributes a large share of total revenue
  * Branded products generate the highest revenue compared to local and imported items

## Business Impact

  * Helps management identify high-performing products and categories
  * Supports better pricing and discount strategies
  * Improves customer experience by analyzing payment and shipping preferences
  * Helps reduce order cancellations through pattern analysis
  * Enables year-wise performance tracking in one dashboard
